They give a boolean depending on the values you give them:

`==`
	Equal to
`!=`
	Not equal to
`<`
	Less than
`>`
	Greater than
`<=`
	Less than or equal to
`>=`
	Greater than or equal to

Don't use the `==` or `!=` operators to evaluate boolean operations.
Use `is` or `is not` operators
